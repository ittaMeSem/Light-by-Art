about us

Welcome to Light by Art!
Through authenticity, simplicity and mindfulness, starting from a small town “homemade” business, we’ve created a design and fine arts company.

Our vision is to cover the needs of even the most complex customers and unleash the world’s creative energy. The happy moments our illustrations and pieces of art bring will unfold a welcoming atmoshpere.

With a motivated team, we strive to be the creative minds that bring a smile to your face. That’s why we’re always looking for innovative new ways to get the best to you.
